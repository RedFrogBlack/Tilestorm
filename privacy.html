Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: CROAKDOWN collects nothing about you. The game runs entirely in your browser. No tracking, no cookies, no ads, no servers storing your data. That's it.

What we collect — Nothing.

The game runs in your browser. Your progress, settings, and best scores are saved in your device's localStorage. Nothing leaves your device. We don't have a backend that stores your data because we don't have a backend at all.

Analytics

None. No Google Analytics. No Firebase. No Plausible. No heatmaps. No session replay. Nothing.

Cookies

None. We don't set any cookies — first-party or third-party.

Ads

None. CROAKDOWN has no advertising and never will. Your attention isn't for sale.

Purchases

Optional tier unlocks are available. Payment processing details are TBC and will be handled by a reputable third-party processor — we won't store your payment information ourselves. We'll update this policy with full details before any paid tiers go live.

Third Parties

The game files are served from GitHub Pages. As standard infrastructure, GitHub may log IP addresses of requests to their servers. See github.com/privacy for details on how they handle that. We don't receive or process those logs.

Children

We collect no data from anyone of any age. CROAKDOWN is safe for all ages.

Your Rights

We hold no data about you, so there's nothing to request, export, or delete on our end. To clear your local game progress, clear your browser's site data for this domain.

Changes

If anything changes about how this works, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above. If we ever start collecting data — which is not the plan — we'll be loud and clear about it.
