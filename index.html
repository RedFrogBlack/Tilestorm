🐸 The Frogs — What They Do
🔴 Red Frog — Adds Tiles
Jumps across the board and drops extra tiles, making it harder to clear lines. Appears more often as your pace picks up.

Hops in and clears tiles from the board, giving you breathing room. A helpful visitor — don't scare it away.
Want to turn the frogs off? Go to ⚙ Settings (bottom of the home screen) → Display section → toggle Red Frog or Purple Frog on/off independently.
